Methodology

Predictions use the Log5 method, a formula developed by Bill James for estimating head-to-head win probabilities from each team's overall winning percentage.

Per-game probability: P(A) = (pA - pA×pB) / (pA + pB - 2×pA×pB) where pA and pB are regular-season point percentages. The higher seed receives a +1.5% bonus for home-ice advantage.

Series probability is computed via the negative binomial distribution — the probability of reaching exactly 4 wins before the opponent does in a best-of-7 format.

Series length is estimated from the dominance differential: lopsided matchups tend to end in fewer games.
